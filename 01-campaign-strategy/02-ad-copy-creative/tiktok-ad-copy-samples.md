# TikTok Paid Ads Campaign Overview

Brand: Stufshoppe (Wadudecom LLC)  
Role: Self-Employed Digital Marketing Manager  
Period: 2023–2024

## Objective
Drive traffic and conversions for an e-commerce brand using TikTok paid advertisements.

## Target Audience
- Age: 18–35
- Interests: Online shopping, trending products, lifestyle content
- Regions: International

## Campaign Structure
- Awareness campaigns (video views, reach)
- Conversion-focused campaigns (website traffic, purchases)

## Budget Management
Daily budgets were monitored and adjusted based on performance metrics such as CTR and conversions.

## Optimization Approach
- Tested multiple creatives and captions
- Paused low-performing ads
- Scaled ads with strong engagement
